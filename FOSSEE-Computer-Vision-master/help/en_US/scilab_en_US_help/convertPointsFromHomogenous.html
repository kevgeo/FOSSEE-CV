convertPointsFromHomogenous

Converts the points from homogeneous to Euclidean space

Calling Sequence

pts_euclidean = convertPointsToHomogeneous(pts_homogenous);

Parameters

pts_homogenous :

vector of N dimensional points in homogenous space

pts_euclidean :

vector of N-1 dimensional points in Euclidean space

Description

The function converts vector of N dimensional points in homogenous space to a vector of N-1 dimensional points in Euclidean space.

Examples

pts_homogenous = [ -75 -90 12;
-120 1 34];
pts_euclidean = convertPointsFromHomogeneous(pts_homogenous);
