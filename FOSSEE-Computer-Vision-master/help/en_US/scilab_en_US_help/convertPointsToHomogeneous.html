convertPointsToHomogeneous

Converts the points from Euclidean to homogeneous type or space.

Calling Sequence

pts_homogenous = convertPointsToHomogeneous(pts_euclidean);

Parameters

pts_euclidean :

vector of N dimensional points in Euclidean space

Description

The function converts vector of N dimensional points in Euclidean space to a vector of N+1 dimensional points in homogenous space.

Examples

//N dimensional points
in = [30 40;
20 10];
//Getting homogenous points
out = convertPointsToHomogeneous(in);
